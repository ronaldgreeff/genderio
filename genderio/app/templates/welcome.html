InstaGender

The fastest, most accurate gender prediction.

How it works

First, a neural network model and trained using thousands of obstetric ultrasound images. Each image is passed into the network with a label of "boy" or "girl".

The network learns what features best distinguish boys from girls to arrive at the correct label.

Once the network is trained, a never-before-seen image can be passed in, and based on what the network has learnt, will output a predicted label of boy or girl.

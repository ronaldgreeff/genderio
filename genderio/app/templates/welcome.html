InstaGender

Free gender prediction

How it works

First, a neural network model and trained using thousands of obstetric ultrasound images. Each image is passed into the network with a label of "boy" or "girl".

Over time, the network determines the best features to distinguish boys and girls and arrive at the correct label. Rather than simply looking at a nub, skull or placenta, neural networks determine what to look for in the "hidden layers" themselves.

Once the network is trained, a never-before-seen image can be passed in, and based on what the network has learnt, output a predicted label of boy or girl.
